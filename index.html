$
I'm a Software Engineer and Researcher based in London, UK.
$
I have just finished my PhD at UCL which has been funded by the Centre for Doctoral Training in Cybersecurity. Here I have been working on fuzzing - a form of automated software testing. Most of the PhD work is on applying fuzzing to the task of detecting leakage of confidential information in programs, though the later work is on strategies to improve program exploration when using a fuzzer to search for any type of bug.
